FAQS

1 What is IICCC?

IICCC stands for INDO INTERNATIONAL CHRISTIAN CHAMBER OF COMMERCE. IICCC is a nodal organization formed to unite the Christian business person, professionals, and leaders of institutions and put them together on one common platform.

3 Why is this organization formed ?

As we all are aware of the current developments that are taking place in various fields in our country and around the world, we are sure that you will agree with us that there is an urgent need to cope with the social and economic developments for the benefit of Christian community at large.The main purpose is to use their - education, experience, expertise, leadership skills, management skills, resources, knowledge, and wisdom of business person, professionls and institutions efficiently, for their own growth and also to support and serve our community to strengthen our nation's growth and prosperity.

5 How can I avail your services?

To access the benefit with IICCC you need to become a member so that you can participate in our activities and get benefited.

7 What are the benefits I can avail as a member?

There is no doubt that you will be benefited. On becoming a member you can participate in meetings in national level, state level and district level such as seminars, workshops etc for business development. Your business will also be promoted through portals, worldwide.

9 What is the profit for IICCC?

IICCC is registered as a nonprofit charitable organisation and serves the society and community without any profit motive.

2 What is the legal framework of IICCC?

IICCC is a company incorporated under Sec25 of the Companies Act, 1956 as a nonprofit charitable organization.

4 What the organization does?

The function of chamber of commerce is to facilitate, inform, promote and serve the community of business person, professional and institution.

6 How do I know the membership fees is used properly?

The Bylaw prevents the use of resources for the benefits of the directors,they cannot claim anything even for transportation. The money is used only for the activities of the organization..

8 What are the functions of the organization?

We facilitate Business person , Professionals, andInstitutional leaders through seminars, workshops, fellowship meets, resource development of all sectors. We provide guidance and counseling to build thei capacity,, conducting trade fairs, exhibitions, and on-demand activities for all three segments .

10 What is the membership fees?

Membership comprises of three types which are,

Platinum (Patron-life)- Rs. 5,00,000

Gold (life)- Rs. 1,00,000

Silver (Annual)- Rs. 20,000.
