History

Indo International Christian Chamber of Commerce (IICCC) is the result of a group of successful Christian businessmen, professionals and leaders of institutions who came together and strongly felt that a nodal organization will have to be formed to unite the Christian businessmen, professionals and leaders of institutions and bring them together on one, common platform. Thus, enabling them to serve, care, give and share their God given talents, knowledge, wisdom, resources, education, experience, expertise and skills, to develop their business, profession and services and to create a network among themselves to connect and contact each other and others by establishing a portal. Thereby, Indo International Christian Chamber of Commerce was formed in the year 2004, as an inter-denominational, non-profit organization registered under Section 25 of the Companies Act 1956, an act of parliament, with a vision to serve not only the Christian community of businessmen, professionals and leaders of institutions but also to serve other communities in extending their Christian love by way of service to uplift every citizen of India – Our Nation.

About IICCC

  • IICCC is a company incorporated under Sec25 of the Companies Act, 1956.
  • IICCC is an inter-denominational organization
  • IICCC serves the society and community without any profit motive. (1)
  • IICCC strives to emphasize true Biblical principles among professionals, business people, and leaders in service and the marketplace.
  • IICCC is a platform that is given to us by our God ALMIGHTY to Christian business people, professionals and leaders of institutions to interlink, serve, receive, give and share their education, experience, expertise, talent, skills, wisdom and knowledge with one another and in turn to serve other communities.
  • IICCC is an organization operating nationwide having a governance structure in Chapters, Districts, and States.
  • IICCC comprises of three groups, Platinum & Gold Members (Life), Annual Members and Associates.
    • Vision

    Our vision is to Unite and create a nation & worldwide network of Christian Businessmen, Professionals & Institutions to share and exchange their education, experience, expertise, skills, knowledge and wisdom to help and strengthen one another to develop the economy of the country and uplift the standard of living of every citizen of India.

    Mission

    Our mission is to interlink, give, receive, enrich, energize, develop, equip, empower, train, serve and transform the businessmen, professionals and institutions with the Christian principles of love and serve in exhibiting the power of God in the market places and in rendering services to one another.

    Purpose

    As we all are aware of the current developments that are taking place in various fields in our country and around the world, we are sure that you will agree with us that there is an urgent need to cope with the social and economic developments for the benefit of our Christian community at large. The need of the hour, especially for the community of Christian businessmen, professionals and leaders of institutions and organizations who are in service to humanity is to use their – education, experience, expertise, leadership skills, management skills, resources, knowledge and wisdom etc. efficiently, not only for their own growth, but also to support and serve our community of every citizen to strengthen our nation’s growth and prosperity.

    Aim & Objectives

    INFORMATION SHARING:

    IICCC is a body in which members invest their time, talent, expertise, experience, education, knowledge, and wisdom to help and promote Christian business people and professionals, and to create skillful, talented leaders.

    IICCC provides guidelines to the members and others on various Rules and Acts of the State and Central Governments.

    NETWORKING

    IICCC enables joint venture collaboration across the globe.

    IICCC has developed a Portal containing details of the business people, professionals, and institutional leaders viz names, addresses, contact information, their activities and areas of expertise to identify one another for development.

    IICCC arranges similar tours from India to other countries for business development and contacts

    CAPACITY BUILDING

    IICCC provides consultations, counseling, and advice to the business people, professionals, and institutions.

    IICCC equips the business people, professionals and leaders of institutions by empowering, training, transforming and rehabilitating them.

    IICCC receives delegations from different parts of the world for business development and community development through institutions.

    RELIEF AND DEVELOPMENT

    IICCC is a facilitator for joint ventures, for the exchange of technical and business know-how for agencies, franchises and such other business arrangements worldwide.

    IICCC handles disaster management programs for outcomes such as cyclones, tsunamis, and earthquakes, etc.

    IICCC acts as a manager, administrator, agent and auditor of funds on behalf of funding agents in India and other Countries.

    To join with NGOs and extend support to achieve their goals and aims to serve the community and to provide them with the necessary infrastructure, guidance, etc.
