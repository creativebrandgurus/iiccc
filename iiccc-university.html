IICCC - On Demand

E-learning that goes beyond your expectations

University of IICCC is now offering remote leadership, finance, project management and managerial skill-development courses you can access from your home... your office... or just about anywhere...anytime. These course take advantage of new technologies and feature engaging distance-learning formats.

If you're swamped during work hours and can't free up time to enhance your skills with our Classroom or Live Online training, university of IICCC has the perfect solution- University of IICCC - On Demand e-learning.

Celebrated authors, professors and management experts-from the most renowned business schools across the global-assisted in developing this superior content, the resulting programs are unlike any distance-learning course ever offered.
