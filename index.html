Welcome to IICCC

Indo International Christian Chamber of Commerce (IICCC) is the result of a group of successful Christian businessmen, professionals & leaders of institutions who came together & strongly felt that a nodal organization will have to be formed to unite the Christian businessmen, professionals & leaders of institutions & bring them together on one, common platform. Thus, enabling them to serve, care, give & share their God given talents, knowledge, wisdom, resources, education, experience, expertise & skills, to develop their business, profession & services & to create a network among themselves to connect & contact each other & others by establishing a portal.

Our Mission

Our mission is to interlink, give, receive, enrich, energize, develop, equip, empower, train, serve & transform the businessmen, professionals & institutions with the Christian principles of love & serve in exhibiting the power of God in the market places & in rendering services to one another.

Our Vision

Our vision is to Unite & create a nation & worldwide network of Christian Businessmen, Professionals & Institutions to share & exchange their education, experience, expertise, skills, knowledge & wisdom to help & strengthen one another to develop the economy of the country & uplift the st&ard of living of every citizen of India.

Our Purpose

As we all are aware of the current developments that are taking place in various fields in our country & around the world, we are sure that you will agree with us that there is an urgent need to cope with the social & economic developments for the benefit of our Christian community at large.

    About IICCC

  • IICCC is a company incorporated under Sec25 of the Companies Act, 1956.
  • IICCC is an inter-denominational organization
  • IICCC serves the society and community without any profit motive. (1)
  • IICCC strives to emphasize true Biblical principles among professionals, business people, and leaders in service and the marketplace.
  • IICCC is a platform that is given to us by our God ALMIGHTY to Christian business people, professionals and leaders of institutions to interlink, serve, receive, give and share their education, experience, expertise, talent, skills, wisdom and knowledge with one another and in turn to serve other communities.
  • IICCC is an organization operating nationwide having a governance structure in Chapters, Districts, and States.
