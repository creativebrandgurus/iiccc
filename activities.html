ACTIVITIES AMONG BUSINESS PEOPLE:

SKILL DEVELOPMENT

SEMINARS & WORKSHOPS ON MANAGEMENT, MARKETING, MANUFACTURING ETC,

AD CAMPAIGN

PROMOTIONAL ACTIVITIES, DIGITAL MARKETING, FACILITATION, INFORMATION AND PROMOTIONAL SERVICES

CONDUCTING EXHIBITIONS, SYMPOSIUM, ETC.

WEEKLY/MONTHLY FELLOWSHIP AND BUSINESS-DEVELOPMENT MEETINGS.

CONDUCT BUSINESS SESSIONS AND DISCUSSIONS AT VARIOUS PLACES, ONCE A MONTH.

RECEIVE, GUIDE AND ASSIST TRADE DELEGATIONS FROM ABROAD.

ORGANIZE AND PROMOTE TRADE DELEGATIONS FROM INDIA TO OTHER COUNTRIES.

COORDINATE WITH OTHER FEDERATIONS LIKE CII, FICCI AND OTHER CHAMBERS OF COMMERCE, GOVERNMENT AGENCIES, AND DEPARTMENTS.

CONDUCT GET-TOGETHERS FOR COUNCILORS, PARLIAMENTARIAN MEETINGS, MEMBERS OF THE LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLIES AND GOVERNMENT OFFICIALS ON VARIOUS OCCASIONS.

ORGANIZE PROGRAMS TO RECOGNIZE OUTSTANDING LEADERS AND TO HONOR THEM WITH AWARDS.

THE ACTIVITIES OF THE CHAMBER GO BEYOND THE TRADITIONAL FORMAT OF INDUSTRY ASSOCIATIONS. WE ASSIST MEMBERS IN PIONEERING THEIR TRADE AND INVESTMENT ACTIVITIES; GUIDE AND ADVISE THEM ON MARKET ACCESS AND PROMOTE THEIR BUSINESSES THROUGH VARIED PLATFORMS SUCH AS CONFERENCES, B2B MEETINGS, SEMINARS AND INTERACTIVE DISCUSSIONS WITH HIGH RANKING OFFICIALS FROM INDIA AND OVERSEAS. THE IICCC ALSO ASSISTS OVERSEAS ENTERPRISES SEEKING BUSINESS OPPORTUNITIES IN INDIA BY IDENTIFYING SUITABLE PARTNERS FOR JOINT VENTURES, TAKEOVERS, AND OTHER COMMERCIAL ALLIANCES.

ACTIVITIES OF IICCC

IICCC REPRESENTS TO THE GOVERNMENT DEPARTMENTS ON BEHALF OF THE PEOPLE

IICCC INFORMS THE NEEDS OF THE PEOPLE TO THE GOVERNMENT AUTHORITIES

IICCC ANALYSES THE REQUIREMENT OF THE PEOPLE AND THE COUNTRY

IICCC INITIATES IN REPORTING FACTS AND FIGURES IN DETAIL STATISTICALLY TALUKWISE, DISTRICTWISE, STATE WISE AND NATIONWIDE

IICCC PROVIDES SUGGESTIONS, GUIDANCE, INFORMATION TO THE GOVERNMENT AUTHORITIES FOR NATIONAL DEVELOPMENT AND BENEFIT OF THE PEOPLE

IICCC CONDUCTS ACTIVITIES & PROJECTS ON EDUCATION, HEALTHCARE & COMMUNITY DEVELOPMENT

IICCC WORKS ON DEVELOPMENT OF BUSINESSMEN, PROFESSIONALS & LEADERS OF THE INSTITUTIONS

IICCC CONDUCTS SKILL DEVELOPMENT COURSES IN EDUCATION, BUSINESS AND OTHER SECTORS

IICCC CONDUCTS AWARENESS PROGRAMS IN HEALTH , EDUCATION, TECHNOLOGY & GOVERNMENT POLICIES

IICCC TAKES INITIATIVES IN SPONSORING THE GOVERNMENT PROGRAMS

IICCC IMPLEMENTS DISASTER MANAGEMENT PROGRAMS IN TIMES OF NEED

IICCC CONDUCTS SEMINARS FOR BUSINESS PEOPLE, PROFESSIONALS, LEADERS OF THE INSTITUTIONS AND FOR THE PUBLIC ON VARIOUS SUBJECTS TO ENRICH AND EXPAND THEIR POTENTIAL

IICCC CONDUCTS TRADE FAIRS, EXHIBITIONS FOR BUSINESS PEOPLE

IICCC CONDUCTS PARLIMENTARIANS MEET ALONG WITH ALL BUSINESSMEN OF THE NATION EVERY YEAR

IICCC TAKES INITIATIVES ON TECHNOLOGY COMMERCIALISATION

IICCC INTERLINKS BUSINESS PEOPLE, PROFESSIONALS & LEADERS OF INSITUTIONS FOR MUTUAL GROWTH BY SHARING THEIR EXPERIENCE, EXPERTISE AND WISDOM AMONG ONE ANOTHER

IICCC INTRODUCES NEW PROJECTS FOR THE DEVELOPMENT OF PEOPLE AND NATION IN TALUKWISE, DISTRICTWISE, STATE WISE AND NATIONWIDE

IICCC TAKES BUSINESS PEOPLE FOR INTERNATIONAL BUSINESS TOUR

IICCC FACILITATES FUND RAISING FOR NGO'S AND INSTITUTIONS THROUGH BUSINESS PEOPLE

IICCC FACILITATES SPONSORSHIP FOR SPORTS DEVELOPMENT AMONG YOUTH

IICCC FACILITATES TO IMPLEMENT MICRO FINANCE IN REQUIRED AREAS OF THE COUNTRY

IICCC CONDUCTS EDUCATION , AWARNESS AND TRAINING PROGRAMS FOR FARMERS ON GLOBAL EXPOSURE IN AGRICULTURE TECHNOLOGY

IICCC INITIATES PROJECTS ON WOMEN ENTERPRUNER DEVELOPMENT

IICCC PROVIDES REASEARCH AND ANALYSIS ON WATER RESOURCE AND MANAGEMENT

IICCC FACILITATES IN TRAINING GOVERMENT AUTHORITIES THROUGH GLOBAL ORGANIZATIONS

IICCC HELPS GOVERMENT IN DEVELOPING HIGH LEVEL INFRASTRUCTURE OF EVERY AREA

IICCC INITIATES AND TAKES EFFORT TO EDUCATE BUSINESS PEOPLE AND GOVERNMENT ON AWARENESS AND IMPLEMENTATION OF LATEST TECHNOLOGIES

IN ALL SECTORS FROM ALL PARTS OF THE WORLD
