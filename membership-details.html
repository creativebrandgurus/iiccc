Benefits for Businessman

Uniting business people and entrepreneurs together, throughout India

Linking the business people with professionals for their better and efficient management

Linking institutions like schools, colleges, hospitals, trusts and societies for rendering service to the poor and needy and to support them in all aspects

Creating opportunities to meet eminent people in industries, trade, and commerce across the nation to participate in exhibitions, workshop, and seminars on different subjects held at different places all over India

To avail joint ventures, technical know-how, collaboration, tie-up agreement, consortium and channel partnership for projects and business

To avail services by entrepreneurs to start micro, small, medium and large scale industries and business enterprises

To help establish micro-financing and other schemes to generate self-employment

To benefit from the Job Seekers’ data available on the portal for employment

To exhibit business activities, products, services and interests via the portal for an All – India and global reach

To get counseling, guidance, advice, feasibility studies, and project appraisals from the panel of experts for the improvement of business and operations

To avail certificate of origin for exporters

To avail of certification for tests - food, educational qualification, license, etc.

To meet the delegates from other countries for business connections

To avail of statistical data on various commodities of imports and exports

Opportunities for expanding the business and availing the resources from the experts across the nation for all

Information updating of various Rules and Acts promulgated by the State and Central governments then and there

Procedures to be followed in establishing the business under Companies Act, Partnership Act, Income Tax Act, etc.

To have a registered forum to effectively represent the grievances, suggestions, and consents of the business and professional community before the State and Central governments

BENEFITS FOR PROFESSIONALS

Linking professionals, namely medical, legal, financial, management, technocrats, consultants and others with business people of all discipliness

Linking professionals with institutions of all types, namely education, NGOs, trusts, societies, etc.

Uniting professionals of each category such as engineers, chartered accountants, doctors, scientists, and others to share their respective experiences and ideas

Agriculture know-how and modern technologies to be made available to agriculturists for better farming

Doctors, advocates, chartered accountants and engineers with their specialization can expand their services among business people and institutions

Scientists’ innovative ideas and valuable suggestions can be a guide for business product development

Other professionals such as musicians, choreographers, painters, photographers and videographers, carpenters, etc., can avail an excellent opportunity to expand their professional contacts through the portal of this chamber

Career opportunities for professionals through “Employment Opportunities” available on the portal

An opportunity for meeting delegates from abroad which would facilitate professional growth

To have an opportunity to meet eminent people in industries, trade, and commerce and in State and Central governments

To represent the grievances and suggestions of professionals with the State and Central governments

To be part of a delegation to visit other countries for professional enrichment

To render services to the institutions to skillfully manage and to follow government procedures such as filing of income tax, FCRA returns, renewal of society registration, etc.

To avail certification for educational qualification, license, etc.

BENEFITS FOR Institutions

Uniting the institutional leaders of all categories, namely schools, colleges, hospitals, trusts, NGOs, etc.

Receive updated information from professionals to skillfully manage and follow government procedures such as filing of Income Tax, FCRA returns, renewal of society registration, etc.

Ambitious educational leaders to take their educational services across the nation

Updating information of various Rules and Acts promulgated by the governments, periodically

Trusts and service organizations can get support from business people to fulfill their vision and render service to the public

Procedural support from professionals like advocates and chartered accountants

Hospitals can come together to render services for the poor and needy

Together with doctors, students and hospitals, research programs can be set up on a large scale for various ailments

Colleges can avail support for their students’ placement program in various corporate, industries and other institutions

An opportunity to meet the delegates from abroad for institutional development in all areas

To upgrade the standard of education wherever possible as per industrial requirements through interaction with professionals and business people

To represent suggestions and grievances before the State and Central government officials and to seek redressal

To form institutions such as schools, hospitals, trusts, NGOs wherever required with guidance from the experts

To avail of certification for educational qualification, license, etc.

To have an opportunity to meet eminent people in industries, trade, and commerce in state and central government.
